The MantaWeb "Remember Me" feature is for those users that wish not to enter their password every time they log into MantaWeb.

The MantaWeb "Remember Me" feature is intended for those individuals that are the only users of their computers or have a seperate login to the computer they use to access this site.

Remember, if you enable this feature, anyone that can access your computer's account will be able to access your MantaWeb account. For maximum security, we suggest that you do not turn this feature on.

To turn the feature off after it has been turned on: click the "I Am Not <your name here>" button, type your login and password, but leave the "Remember Who I Am" checkbox unchecked. Click the login button to log in. Next time you login, MantaWeb will not remember who you are.
